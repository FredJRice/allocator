How to use Court Queue

  • Click on Add Players.
  • Tap player to add to queue, or select Add All.
  • Tap a court to auto-fill or drag player to court.
  • Timers start/stop automatically.
  • To add next players to a court, tap the court again.
  • To remove a player from queue or court, tap 'x'.
  • To edit or delete a player from the pool tap 'x'.
  • Max 4 players per court. Add two and then drag more as needed.
  • Settings > Change number of courts - Max 20.
  • Settings > Modify court timer warning colours.
  • Use Clear All to reset courts & queue.
